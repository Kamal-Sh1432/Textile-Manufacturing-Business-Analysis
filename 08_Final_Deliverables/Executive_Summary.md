## Executive Summary

This engagement provided senior management with a clear, consolidated view of operational performance across production, quality, dispatch, inventory, and maintenance.

By validating data, defining meaningful KPIs, and presenting insights in a business-friendly manner, the analysis helped shift discussions from symptoms to root causes.

The dashboards and recommendations enable management to:
- Identify operational bottlenecks quickly
- Prioritize corrective actions based on data
- Improve efficiency, reduce losses, and optimize working capital
- Use analytics as a decision-support tool rather than a reporting exercise

Overall, this engagement demonstrates how a structured business analytics approach can directly support operational improvement and strategic decision-making.
