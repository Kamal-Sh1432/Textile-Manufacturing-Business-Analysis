## Stakeholder Objectives

The engagement involved multiple stakeholders, each with specific expectations from the analytics initiative.

### Managing Director (MD)
- Gain a clear, consolidated view of overall operational performance
- Understand whether business issues are isolated or systemic
- Identify priority areas requiring immediate corrective action
- Support strategic decisions using reliable, data-backed insights

### Operations Head
- Understand gaps between planned and actual production
- Identify capacity constraints and process inefficiencies
- Reduce unplanned downtime and improve production consistency

### Quality Head
- Identify processes contributing most to rework and wastage
- Understand quality loss trends over time
- Support initiatives to reduce material loss and reprocessing

### Dispatch & Supply Chain Head
- Identify causes of dispatch delays
- Understand whether delays are operational or planning-related
- Improve delivery reliability and customer commitment adherence

### Finance Team
- Monitor inventory levels and working capital exposure
- Understand cost leakage due to inefficiencies
- Support margin improvement initiatives through better operational visibility

Each stakeholder objective was mapped to specific KPIs and dashboards to ensure that analytics outputs directly supported business decisions.
