## Data Sources Overview

This engagement used operational data sourced from the companyâ€™s internal systems.  
The data represents daily transactional and master records required to analyze production, quality, dispatch, inventory, and maintenance performance.

---

### Production Data
- **Source:** Production execution system
- **Granularity:** Daily, per product and process
- **Purpose:** Compare planned vs actual production and identify execution gaps

---

### Rework & Wastage Data
- **Source:** Quality and production logs
- **Granularity:** Daily, per process
- **Purpose:** Identify quality-related losses and process inefficiencies

---

### Dispatch Data
- **Source:** Dispatch and logistics records
- **Granularity:** Daily dispatch activity
- **Purpose:** Analyze delivery delays and dispatch performance

---

### Inventory Data
- **Source:** Inventory management system
- **Granularity:** Daily inventory snapshot
- **Purpose:** Monitor inventory holding behavior and working capital exposure

---

### Machine & Maintenance Data
- **Source:** Maintenance logs
- **Granularity:** Event-based
- **Purpose:** Identify machines contributing to unplanned downtime

---

### Cost Data
- **Source:** Finance and cost accounting records
- **Granularity:** Monthly
- **Purpose:** Support inventory valuation and cost impact analysis
