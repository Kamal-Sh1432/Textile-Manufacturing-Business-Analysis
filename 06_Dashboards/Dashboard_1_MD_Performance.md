### Purpose
This dashboard provides the Managing Director with a single-page, high-level view of overall operational performance.  
It is designed for quick interpretation and supports daily and monthly decision-making.

---

### Key Business Questions Addressed
- Are we meeting production plans?
- Where are quality losses occurring?
- Are dispatch delays isolated or systemic?
- How efficiently is inventory being managed?

---

### KPIs Displayed
- Planned Production (KG)
- Actual Production (KG)
- Production Achievement (%)
- Rework (%)
- Wastage (%)
- Dispatch Delay Rate (%)
- Inventory Holding Days (Average)

---

### Visual Components
- **KPI Cards:** Instant snapshot of current performance
- **Production Trend Line:** Planned vs Actual production over time
- **Rework & Wastage by Process:** Identifies quality leakage areas
- **Inventory Trend:** Tracks inventory holding behavior
- **Dispatch Performance Chart:** Highlights dispatch planning vs execution

---

### Design Considerations
- Minimal clutter with a clear visual hierarchy
- Business-friendly labels instead of technical terms
- Consistent color usage to support quick scanning
- Filters for time period, product, and process

---

### Business Value
This dashboard enables senior management to:
- Quickly identify operational gaps
- Focus discussions on priority problem areas
- Monitor performance trends without deep data analysis
