## Business Recommendations

Based on the insights derived from the analysis, the following actions were recommended to management.

---

### Production Planning
- Align production plans with realistic machine capacity
- Introduce weekly rolling plans instead of static monthly targets

---

### Quality Control
- Implement first-batch quality approvals in high-risk processes
- Track rework and wastage reasons on a daily basis

---

### Dispatch Management
- Review dispatch planning assumptions
- Improve coordination between production and logistics teams

---

### Inventory Optimization
- Monitor inventory holding days at daily granularity
- Define minimum and maximum stock thresholds

---

### Maintenance Strategy
- Identify high-downtime machines and prioritize preventive maintenance
- Schedule maintenance during low-load production periods
