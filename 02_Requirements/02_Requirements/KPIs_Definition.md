## KPI Definitions

This document outlines the key performance indicators used in the engagement and their business relevance.

---

### Planned Production (KG)
Total quantity planned for production within a given period.

**Business Purpose:**  
Provides the baseline against which execution performance is measured.

---

### Actual Production (KG)
Total quantity actually produced within a given period.

**Business Purpose:**  
Indicates true production output and execution capability.

---

### Production Achievement (%)
Actual Production divided by Planned Production.

**Business Purpose:**  
Measures how effectively production plans are being met.

---

### Rework (%)
Percentage of produced quantity that requires reprocessing.

**Business Purpose:**  
Indicates quality issues and operational inefficiencies.

---

### Wastage (%)
Percentage of material lost during the production process.

**Business Purpose:**  
Highlights material loss impacting cost and margins.

---

### Dispatch Delay Rate (%)
Percentage of dispatches that were delayed.

**Business Purpose:**  
Identifies delivery reliability issues and systemic dispatch problems.

---

### Inventory Holding Days
Average number of days current inventory can support production.

**Calculation Approach:**  
Calculated at daily granularity using daily inventory and daily production, then averaged to avoid cumulative distortion.

**Business Purpose:**  
Monitors working capital exposure and inventory efficiency.

---

### Machine Downtime (Hours)
Total hours of unplanned machine downtime.

**Business Purpose:**  
Identifies machines contributing most to production delays.
